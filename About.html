Who is Lone Ranger Mechanics

Lone Ranger Mechanics is a mobile repair and inspection service specializing in heavy machinery, farm equipment and automotive work. Based in Oklahoma City, we’re built for the hard‑working industries that keep America moving—construction, agriculture, transportation and beyond.

Founded on the principles of reliability, honesty and hands‑on experience, we bring professional‑grade mechanical services directly to your job site, farm or home. Whether you’re dealing with a downed dozer, a tractor that won’t start or a truck that needs immediate attention, we show up ready to work with the right tools, knowledge and commitment to get you back up and running fast.

We don’t just fix machines—we build trust through every job. Our goal is to minimize your downtime, extend the life of your equipment and provide convenient service you can count on.

When you call Lone Ranger Mechanics, you’re not just getting a mechanic—you’re getting a partner in your operation.
